The beach hotel and restaurant, what can we do for you ?

Welcome to The Beach Hotel

We are an apprentice run (with a little help from us) fourteen-room hotel in Minehead with the beach and South West Coast Path on our doorstep. Our beautiful double rooms showcase the views of our heritage railway, Exmoor and the sea front. We are proud to promote local produce throughout our hotel. Whether you are looking for a relaxing beach getaway, or you need to place to rest your head after a day of outdoor adventures, we are here to help!

Our apprentices are working with our Head Chef Angus to create the perfect menu for our guests and can’t wait to showcase their skills.

Our Rooms

Our bright and fresh seaside rooms with a distinct New England vibe, will help you kick back, relax and enjoy the views. With a range of rooms to suit your needs you will always have a stay to remember.

Food & Drink

Our award winning Galley restaurant and kitchen theatre will always surprise you deliciously different dishes and a twist of something new, watch our chefs work and plate up in front of your eyes, stunning dishes in a lovely environment.

Occasions

We can help make any occasion the perfect one. Whether it’s your Wedding, another celebration or a meeting, we’d be delighted to cater for all your needs!
